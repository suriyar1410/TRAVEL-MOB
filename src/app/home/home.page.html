Highlights

1. Infinity Pool

World-Famous Pool: The SkyPark’s infinity pool is the world’s largest rooftop pool, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the Singapore skyline.

Exclusive Access: Note that the pool is only accessible to hotel guests, making it a special experience for those staying at Marina Bay Sands.

2. Observation Deck

Stunning Views: The public observation deck, located on the 57th floor, provides sweeping 360-degree views of Singapore, including iconic landmarks like the Gardens by the Bay, the Singapore Flyer, and the Marina Bay waterfront.

Photo Opportunities: Perfect for capturing stunning photos of the cityscape, especially at sunset.

3. Dining

Sky-High Restaurants: The SkyPark features several high-end dining options, including CÉ LA VI and Spago by Wolfgang Puck, where you can enjoy gourmet meals while taking in the views.

Sunset Drinks: The bars are great for enjoying a cocktail while watching the sunset over the city.

4. Architecture

Iconic Design: The SkyPark’s unique design, resembling a ship perched atop three towers, is a marvel of modern architecture and engineering.

Garden in the Sky: The park also features landscaped gardens with over 250 trees and 650 plants, providing a serene escape above the bustling city.

5. Light Shows

Spectra – A Light & Water Show: Visible from the SkyPark, this nightly multimedia show at Marina Bay Sands is a visual feast with water fountains, lasers, and music.
